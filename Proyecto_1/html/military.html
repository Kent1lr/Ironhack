MILITARY-INSPIRED STYLE, OR MILITARIA

Above all, military clothing is designed to last. Fans of thrift and vintage have understood this.

That's why militaria was born. A love of vintage military items that last over time, have a life and a history. A passion for garments that have acquired a patina over time.

These items can be either reproductions of military items, or real clothes that belonged to soldiers from different countries (often the USA). There are also uniforms that have never been used, having been stored in boxes in as-new condition (known as NOS).

Some emblematic pieces from this universe: the famous Jungle Jacket, the gurkha pants and the OG-107 shirt!
