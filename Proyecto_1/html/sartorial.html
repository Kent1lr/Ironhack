THE BEST DRESSED: SARTORIAL STYLE, OR SARTORIALISM

Sartorial style is classic masculine elegance.

Far from today's dress codes, which have completed and buried the wearing of suits, sartorial style, on the contrary, makes them its leitmotif. Don't talk about t-shirts, sneakers or jeans here.

The pure sartorialist will - at the risk of shocking or displeasing - only eat fine shoes, fine shirts with mother-of-pearl buttons, and will see in his sartorial approach the worthy descendant of men from another time, from the aristocrats of the 16th century to the New Yorkers of the 60s.

Sartorial style in one outfit: a wool-silk suit with generous lapels, complemented by pants with darts and tabs. A textured tie completes the outfit.
