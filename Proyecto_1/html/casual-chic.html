THE MOST COMMON: CASUAL-CHIC

Casual-chic is to men's wardrobes what cherry tomatoes are to aperitifs. A safe bet. Guaranteed not to shock anyone. In fact, it's guaranteed to please everyone.

It's the one most men will want to adopt. The one most women will appreciate. It's the assurance of getting through the obligations of everyday life without a hitch, while being noticed, but not too much.

A cocktail party, a job interview, or a Sunday visit to the in-laws? Casual-chic style is often your most precious ally.

The most emblematic pieces: a chino, a shirt, a pair of derbies and a blazer.
